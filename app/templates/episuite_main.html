About EPI Suite

EPI Suite™ is a suite of physical/chemical property and environmental fate estimation programs developed by the US EPA.

Currently Supported Modules:
  • KOWWIN - Octanol-water partition coefficient (Log Kow)
  • BIOWIN - Biodegradation probability (Biowin1–Biowin6)
  • BCFBAF - Bioconcentration & bioaccumulation factors
  • KOCWIN - Soil sorption coefficient (Koc)
Planned Modules:
  • WSKOW - Water solubility
  • AOPWIN - Atmospheric oxidation
  • HYDROWIN - Hydrolysis rates
  • KOCWIN - Soil sorption coefficient
  • HENRY - Henry's Law constant
